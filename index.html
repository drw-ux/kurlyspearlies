IThe Philosophy

A dental visit can be a place to exhale — not endure.

Most hygiene appointments are built around a thirty-minute turnover. Kurly's Pearlies is built around the opposite belief: that real care takes time, careful hands, and a room that doesn't smell like a clinic.

Kurly's Pearlies is an independent dental hygiene clinic in Georgetown, Ontario, owned and operated by Kelly Smith, a Registered Dental Hygienist. We serve clients from Halton Hills, Milton, Oakville, Brampton, Mississauga, Etobicoke, Burlington, Guelph, and Hamilton.

IIIThe Founder

A practice of one.

Hi, I'm Kelly Smith — a Registered Dental Hygienist for over eight years, and the founder of Kurly's Pearlies. In 2024 I left the conventional clinic world to build a quieter, more thoughtful one — a single chair, a single hygienist, and the time to do it properly.

What I love most is watching people leave with their confidence restored. Helping you feel calm, informed, and unhurried is the entire point of this small place.
