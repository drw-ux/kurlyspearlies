At-Home Maintenance Georgetown · Ontario

If you have sensitive teeth, want a more gradual brightening, or just love the routine of doing it at home, custom Philips whitening trays are the answer. Made from impressions of your own teeth, they hold the whitening gel exactly where it should be — no leaks, no gum irritation, no guessing.

How it works.

  1. A 20–30 minute impression appointment (upper + lower)
  2. Trays made to fit your teeth exactly
  3. Pickup + gel choice — DayWhite (30–60 min daily) or NiteWhite (overnight)
  4. Wear daily for 1–2 weeks

The best combo

ZOOM whitening for the big result, trays for once-or-twice-a-year touch-ups. Pair either with a fresh cleaning for the brightest outcome.

Frequent asks.

How are the trays made?

A 20–30 minute impression appointment captures moulds of your upper and lower teeth. Trays are made to fit exactly, then you pick them up with your choice of gel.

DayWhite or NiteWhite — which should I pick?

DayWhite suits busy schedules: 30–60 minutes during the day with lower sensitivity. NiteWhite is worn overnight and suits heavy stainers who want deeper results.

How long does the gel last?

A standard kit covers the initial 1–2 week course plus touch-ups. Most clients wear trays once or twice a year afterward to maintain their shade.

Can I order refill gel?

Yes — refill syringes of DayWhite or NiteWhite are available anytime. Your trays last for years, so maintenance costs only the gel.

Will custom trays work if I have crowns?

Whitening gel only lightens natural enamel — crowns, veneers, and fillings keep their original shade. We assess your smile at the impression appointment so you know what to expect.
